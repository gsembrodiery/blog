Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

"Every thread we stitch carries the soul of Garividi — our heritage, our pride, our craft."

Born in the heart of Garividi, Vizianagaram District, GS Computer Embroidery is a family-run studio where traditional craftsmanship meets modern embroidery technology. We've been adorning families across Andhra Pradesh with our handcrafted and machine-embroidered garments.

From delicate floral machine embroideries on kurtis to bold custom motifs on contemporary garments, every piece from our studio is stitched with love, precision, and a deep respect for textile artistry.
